WHAT WAS THE FIRST GOOGLE EASTER EGG?
Well, the first Google easter egg was made in 1998.
But, sadly, this Google easter egg no longer exists.
This is what it did:
If you searched "Google" in the search bar, it would say "Did you mean... BackRub?"
WHY?
Well, Google's original name was "BackRub". But, they renamed it to Google.
-----------------
WAS GOOGLE ACTUALLY NAMED GOOGLE?
No...it was a mistake.
What happened was that they were going to name it Googol.
If you search Googol in the search, that equals a BIG number.
That qualifies that this searching engine had a lot of information.
But, when they registered the domain name, they accidentally spelled "ol" into "le".
That turned Googol into Google.
