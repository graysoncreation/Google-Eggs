This is where we learn about the Google Easter Eggs.
1. Go to elgoog.com to get easter Eggs.
2. Go to chrome://dino to play the dino game. ONLY COMPUTER!
3. Search askew for a tilted screen.
4. Search "do a barrel roll" for your screen to spin.
5. Search cats or dogs to press the paw button and see them!
6. Search "tic tac toe" to play Tic Tac Toe.
7. Search "flip a coin" to flip a coin.
8. Search "pacman" in order to get the Pac-Man Doodle game.
9. Search "solar eclipse" to get a solar eclipse animation.
10. Search "best font ever" to change Google's font.
11. Search these things to change your font:
"Permanent marker font"
"impact font"
12. Search "Cha Cha Slide" and click the microphone button.
13. Search "the number of horns on a unicorn" to get a calculator with 1 on it.
14. Search "what sound does a "pick an animal" make?" to get a sound button that makes the sound of the animal you put.
15. Search "minecraft" for a minecraft button at the bottom of you screen to MINE GOOGLE!
16. If you search anything and then press "I'm Feeling Lucky" then it brings you to what you are looking for.
17. If you search "christmas" and press the present, you find Santa Tracker.
18. Search "fidget spinner" to get a fidget spinner on your screen.
19. Search "solitare" to play solitare.
